    • Sinso Medical Imaging DAPP
    Sinso Medical Imaging DAPP is a medical image medical record storage and consultation platform based on Filecoin
    Sinso Donors Network
    Sinso Donors Network is an ecosystem that let users to actively participate in valuable medical data sharing and contribution. It provides a set of incentive agreements for medical data sharing and contribution of data assets.
    Sinso Earnings Getway
    The Sinso Earnings Getway is a smart contract system that automatically allocation of finance income for miners and related participants, including a blockchain browser.
    Sinso DAC
    Sinso DAC is a company structure running on a block chain, using smart contracts to ensure its transparency and fairness, Sinso DAC is for participants and investors to build an environment of freedom and equality, and mostly driven by intelligent contracts. Sinso DAC is also a community project, and non-boundary company, we encourage more and more people to join Sinso's business.
