    About us

    It is a great honor to join Filecoin Frontier Accelerator. Our team is from Chengdu, China , our project name is "Sinso Medical Imaging", our team has 5 years of development background in the medical industry, and the previous applications we made is based on traditional cloud computing, but we cannot stop our career. We have always been wanted to explore the applications with web3 architecture.


    And a complete Filecoin-based medical imaging APP will be developed, which will have an online diagnosis function built in, allowing patients to record their medical records in the filecoin network during the diagnosis and treatment process, in addition to permanently storing their key medical data, more importantly, we will assign a private key to customers according to the asymmetric encryption account system, allowing them to completely control their medical data. This seemingly simple APP creates a "patient-centered" ecology.


    After that, we will use the blockchain token incentive method to encourage patients to actively maintain their medical data. We look forward to helping users actively participate for their health, and this will be a very cool application. thank you all!
